Cosmic BMI Questions

Body Mass Index (BMI) is a numerical value calculated from your weight and height. It provides a simple way to categorize individuals into weight classes that may indicate health risks.

While BMI is a useful screening tool, it doesn't directly measure body fat. Athletes with high muscle mass may have high BMI without excess fat. Older adults may have normal BMI but reduced muscle mass.

For overweight individuals, combining a balanced diet with regular physical activity is key. For underweight individuals, focus on nutrient-dense foods and strength training. Always consult with a healthcare professional.
